### FAQ SECTION

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What documents do I need for tax preparation?**
A: For individual returns, bring W-2s, 1099s, last year's tax return, Social Security numbers for all family members, and documentation for any deductions (mortgage interest, charitable donations, medical expenses, etc.). For businesses, bring profit & loss statements, balance sheets, and all income/expense documentation.

**Q: How much do your services cost?**
A: Our pricing depends on the complexity of your situation. We offer transparent pricing and will provide a quote before beginning any work. Contact us for a free estimate.

**Q: Do I need to come to your office?**
A: Not necessarily! We offer virtual consultations and can work with you remotely. Simply upload your documents through our secure client portal, and we'll handle the rest.

**Q: What if I receive an IRS notice?**
A: Don't panic, and don't ignore it. Contact us immediately. We'll review the notice, explain what it means, and handle the response on your behalf.

**Q: Can you help with past-due tax returns?**
A: Absolutely. We specialize in helping clients get caught up on unfiled returns and can work with the IRS to resolve any outstanding issues.

**Q: Do you work with businesses outside my area?**
A: Yes! We work with clients nationwide. Thanks to secure technology, we can serve you regardless of your location.
