## IRS RESOLUTION SERVICES

### Expert Tax Problem Resolution

Facing IRS tax problems can be overwhelming and stressful. Our comprehensive IRS resolution services help you resolve tax issues, protect your assets, and regain peace of mind. We handle everything from simple notice responses to complex tax debt negotiations.

**Our IRS Resolution Services:**

### 1. IRS Notice Response
- **Review and Explain IRS Letters** – Decode confusing IRS correspondence and explain what it means for you
- **Draft Professional Responses** – Prepare accurate, timely responses to IRS inquiries
- **Direct IRS Communication** – Communicate with the IRS on your behalf so you don't have to

### 2. Installment Agreements (Payment Plans)
- **Monthly Payment Plans** – Set up affordable payment arrangements to pay off tax debt over time
- **Negotiate Payment Terms** – Work with the IRS to secure the lowest possible monthly payment
- **Reinstate Defaulted Agreements** – Assist with reinstating payment plans that have lapsed

### 3. Offer in Compromise (OIC) Assistance
- **Eligibility Determination** – Assess if you qualify to settle your tax debt for less than owed
- **OIC Application Preparation** – Prepare and submit complete Offer in Compromise applications
- **Documentation & Negotiation** – Gather supporting documents and negotiate with the IRS

### 4. Penalty Abatement Requests
- **First-Time Penalty Abatement** – Request penalty relief for taxpayers with good compliance history
- **Reasonable Cause Relief** – Document circumstances that justify penalty removal
- **Professional IRS Communication** – Submit abatement requests and follow up with the IRS

### 5. Wage Garnishment & Levy Release
- **Immediate Levy Release** – Request emergency release of wage garnishments and bank levies
- **Payment Alternatives** – Negotiate alternatives to forced collection actions
- **Future Levy Prevention** – Put protections in place to prevent future levies

### 6. Tax Lien Assistance
- **Lien Withdrawal Requests** – Request removal of federal tax liens from your credit report
- **Lien Subordination** – Allow other creditors to move ahead of the IRS lien
- **Lien Release Support** – Secure release of liens after tax debt is paid or settled

### 7. Back Tax Filing & Compliance
- **Prepare Unfiled Tax Returns** – Complete and file past-due tax returns to stop IRS enforcement
- **Correct Previously Filed Returns** – Amend returns with errors or missing information
- **Full Compliance Restoration** – Bring you back into complete compliance with IRS requirements

### 8. Innocent Spouse Relief
- **Eligibility Evaluation** – Determine if you qualify for relief from your spouse's tax debt
- **Relief Request Preparation** – Prepare and submit innocent spouse relief applications
- **Review Process Support** – Guide you through the IRS review and decision process

### 9. Audit Support & Representation
- **Audit Notice Response** – Respond to IRS audit notifications promptly and professionally
- **Documentation Preparation** – Gather and organize records to support your tax return positions
- **IRS Auditor Communication** – Represent you in all communications with IRS auditors

### 10. Currently Not Collectible (CNC) Status
- **Eligibility Determination** – Assess if you qualify for temporary collection suspension
- **Hardship Documentation** – Prepare detailed financial hardship documentation
- **CNC Status Request** – Submit requests to place your account in Currently Not Collectible status

### 11. IRS Account Transcripts Review
- **Transcript Retrieval** – Pull and analyze your IRS account transcripts
- **Issue Identification** – Identify problems, outstanding balances, and critical deadlines
- **Resolution Planning** – Create a comprehensive action plan to resolve all issues

### 12. State Tax Resolution Services
- **State Tax Notices** – Respond to notices from state revenue departments
- **State Payment Plans** – Negotiate payment arrangements with state tax authorities
- **State Liens & Levies** – Resolve state tax liens, levies, and wage garnishments

**Why Choose Our IRS Resolution Services:**
- We communicate directly with the IRS on your behalf – you never face them alone
- Experienced in negotiating favorable outcomes with tax authorities
- We protect your rights and work to minimize your tax liability
- Comprehensive solutions for all types of tax problems
- Handle both federal (IRS) and state tax issues

**Who Needs IRS Resolution Services:**
- Taxpayers with unfiled tax returns
- Anyone receiving IRS notices or letters
