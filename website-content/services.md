## IRS AUDIT REPRESENTATION

### Your Voice at the IRS

Receiving a notice from the IRS can be stressful and intimidating. You don't have to face it alone. Our experienced team provides professional representation before the IRS to protect your rights and resolve your case.

**Our Audit Defense Services:**
- **IRS Correspondence Audits** – We respond to IRS letters and requests for documentation on your behalf
- **Office & Field Audit Representation** – We accompany you to IRS meetings or represent you in your absence
- **Appeals Representation** – If you disagree with an IRS decision, we'll represent you in the appeals process
- **Offer in Compromise (OIC)** – Negotiate to settle your tax debt for less than what you owe
- **Installment Agreement Negotiation** – Set up manageable payment plans for outstanding tax balances
- **Penalty Abatement Requests** – Request removal of penalties due to reasonable cause
- **Innocent Spouse Relief** – Protection for spouses unfairly held liable for a partner's tax debt
- **IRS Levy & Lien Release** – Work to release wage garnishments and property liens

**Why Choose Us for Audit Defense:**
- We communicate directly with the IRS on your behalf
- You never have to speak to an IRS agent alone
- We know your rights and how to protect them
- We work to achieve the best possible resolution
